The Rhythm of Democracy

Gamified democracy respects your time and energy. It is not an exhausting, continuous demand. It is built on a natural, seasonal rhythm of participation called Civic Surges. We gather to solve challenges, we upgrade our systems, and then we rest.

🌊

Major Surges

Massive, highly-coordinated events where the entire community focuses its intelligence.

  • › The March Surge: Our most important annual event. We formulate a comprehensive "Shadow Budget" in March, giving us a full month to shape the Federal Budget in May.
  • › Election Campaigns: Surges activated during federal or state election cycles to mobilise the Cyber Republic.
💧

Minor Surges

The gentle, ongoing pulse of local stewardship. The exact timing is entirely up to you.

  • › Community Voted Cadence: Your local area decides the rhythm. It could be one week a month, two dedicated weekends, or aligned with school and public holidays.
  • › Fractional Participation: You only vote on the local issues you care about, preventing burnout while maintaining community care.

A visualisation of a healthy democratic pulse: focused surges of activity followed by essential periods of rest and system integration.

We aim to value the foundational work that traditional economics ignores. By introducing the Community-Hour (C-Hour), we can unlock and reward the immense value found in civic care, local agriculture, and ecological restoration.

We hold ourselves to a standard of truth that legacy politics cannot match. Inspired by the Welsh Senedd model, we are adopting strict anti-lying paradigms and open Automated Decision Making (ADM) logs to guarantee absolute transparency.
