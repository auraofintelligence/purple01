Set-Piece Civic Surges

Gamified democracy is not about exhausting our communities with continuous, daily demands. It is built on the concept of the Civic Surge. We gather, we focus our collective intelligence to solve specific local or national challenges, we upgrade the system, and then we rest.

1. Preparation Phase

Spec-driven policies are drafted openly on GitHub-style repositories. Communities review the data quietly at their own pace.

2. The Civic Surge

A focused, joyful, and gamified event (e.g. over a weekend). Communities collaborate, vote, and allocate resources to solve the specific brief.

3. Rest & Integration

The software updates. Communities return to their lives, trusting the automated transparency of the system while the new policy takes effect.
